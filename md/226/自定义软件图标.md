1. 应用的配置文件目录是：

> /usr/share/applications

2. 配置文件的标准格式是：


```bash
[Desktop Entry]
Name=phpstorm
Name[zh_CN]=phpstorm
Comment=phpsotorm
Exec=/home/br/Application/phpstorm/bin/phpstorm.sh
Icon=/home/br/Application/phpstorm/bin/phpstorm.svg
Terminal=false
Type=Application
Categories=Application;
Encoding=UTF-8
StartupNotify=true
```
